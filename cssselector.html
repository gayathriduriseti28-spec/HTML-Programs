DORAEMON

About Us

Doraemon is a Japanese cartoon (anime and manga) about a robotic cat from the 22nd century. He travels back in time to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi using futuristic gadgets from his magical pocket.

Main Characters

Doraemon, Nobita, Shizuka, Gian, Suneo, Dorami, and many more.

Doraemon is loved worldwide for its fun stories, amazing gadgets, and the strong friendship between Doraemon and Nobita.
