Miraculous Ladybug

About Show

Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir is a French animated series about two teenagers, Marinette and Adrien, who transform into superheroes Ladybug and Cat Noir to protect Paris.

Main Characters

Ladybug (Marinette)

Cat Noir (Adrien)

Hawk Moth (Gabriel Agreste)

Kwamis are magical creatures that give powers.

Ladybug’s kwami is Tikki, and Cat Noir’s kwami is Plagg.

Together, they fight villains and save Paris.
